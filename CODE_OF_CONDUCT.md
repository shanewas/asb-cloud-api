# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a respectful, practical place to collaborate. Participants are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent when possible, and focus disagreement on ideas and code.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Public or private abuse based on identity, background, or experience level.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact a maintainer privately with relevant context.
